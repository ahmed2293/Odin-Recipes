Crepe

Chicken Crepe is one of the most growing recipes in Egypt for the last ten years.

It's so satisfyingly delicious that you can not get it out of your mind.

Ingredients

  • all purpose flour
  • water
  • Chicken breast
  • Ketchup
  • your favourite kinds of cheese
  • olives and bell peppers

Steps

  1. Start by making your salty Crepe bread
  2. Cut your chicken breast into small cubes and grill it the way you prefer
  3. put your chicken and vigges onto the crepe bread
  4. add your Ketchup and cheese
  5. wrap your Sandwich and put it on the grill for a few minutes on each side till your cheese melts
  6. Enjoy your meal and wish me succes in web development
